Nina-Simone is an independent filmmaker who creates films for the enjoyment of herself and others. She believes cinema is the most unique form of storytelling. Along with filmmaking, Nina-Simone has experience with photography, film editing, and music software such as Logic Pro X. Nina-Simone has won numerous awards and is featured on IMDb. Nina-Simone attended JP Stevens High School where she was a Film Club board member, JPS Regalis Yearbook Club member, and JPS Hawkeye school newspaper member. Now, she currently attends Eugene Lang at The New School where she majors in filmmaking. Nina-Simone has also partnered with Cinema Ed on multiple occasions and has mentored students from Orange High School for the past two years.
